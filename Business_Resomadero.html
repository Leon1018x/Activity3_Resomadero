AnimeNexus Theater


Founded on 2018


Welcome to AnimeNexus Theater, where the vibrant world of Japanese animation comes to life on the big screen! Founded in 2018, Anime Cinema has been dedicated to bringing the best of anime to audiences worldwide, showcasing a diverse range of genres and captivating storytelling. Join us as we dive into the imaginative realms of anime, where every frame is a masterpiece waiting to be explored.

Available Movies


The Boy and the Heron

"The Boy and the Heron" is a touching story of friendship between a lonely boy and a majestic heron, set in a serene lakeside village.


Demon Slayer: Kimetsu No Yaiba - To the Hashira Training

"Demon Slayer: Kimetsu No Yaiba - To the Hashira Training" follows protagonist Tanjiro's intense training with the elite Hashira, as he hones his skills to battle powerful demons and protect humanity.


A Whisker Away

"A Whisker Away" is a charming tale of a girl who transforms into a cat to be closer to her crush, navigating the complexities of love, friendship, and self-discovery in a whimsical adventure.


Your Name

"Your Name" is a captivating story of two strangers who find themselves inexplicably connected through their dreams, leading to a profound journey of love, fate, and the transcendence of time itself.


Weathering with You

"Weathering With You" is a captivating tale of a boy who discovers a girl with the ability to control the weather, leading to a journey of love, destiny, and the consequences of altering nature.
